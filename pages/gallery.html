Our Goals

Research, preserve, represent and popularize art. To form cultural identity, drawing the attention of society to the important role that art as a whole plays in it. To make people's lives better by opening up wide access to the masterpieces of world art.

Gallery Portfolio

ART Gallery is a gallery of contemporary art, showing works by artists of national importance, and represents and supports promising artists in their professional artistic way. The gallery is run over 20 years in the art world, during which it had specialized in identifying, sale and purchase of national arts, and art collections establishment for collectors on abroad.
